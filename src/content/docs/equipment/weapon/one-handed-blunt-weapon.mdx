One-handed blunt weapons like maces deliver powerful crushing blows while allowing for shield usage or dual-wielding.

## Weapon Stats

* **Attack Speed:** Normal (5)
* **Weapon Attack:** 80~150
* **Req. Level:** 20~200 (varies by tier)
* **Req. STR:** High
* **Knockback Chance:** Moderate

## Class Usage

Primarily used by:
* Warriors (especially Crusaders and Paladins)
* Some Pirates (for high damage output)
* Can be dual-wielded by certain classes

## Special Features

* Higher damage than one-handed swords
* Increased chance to knockback enemies
* Some weapons provide bonus HP
* Certain weapons have stun effects
* Good for bossing due to high damage

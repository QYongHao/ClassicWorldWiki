Bows are ranged weapons that provide balanced attack power and range. They are the primary weapons for bowmen.

## Weapon Stats

* **Attack Speed:** Normal (5)
* **Weapon Attack:** 70~150
* **Req. Level:** 20~200 (varies by tier)
* **Req. DEX:** High
* **Attack Range:** Long

## Class Usage

Primarily used by:
* Bowmen (all specializations)
* Some hybrid classes with ranged attacks

## Special Features

* Good balance of damage and attack speed
* Some bows provide bonus accuracy
* Certain bows have elemental attributes
* Can be used with arrow skills
* Excellent for mobbing due to range

Two-handed axes deliver devastating cleaving attacks. They offer a balance between damage and attack speed among two-handed weapons.

## Weapon Stats

* **Attack Speed:** Normal (5)
* **Weapon Attack:** 90~170
* **Req. Level:** 20~200 (varies by tier)
* **Req. STR:** High
* **Critical Chance:** Moderate

## Class Usage

Primarily used by:
* Warriors (especially Berserkers and Dark Knights)
* Some Pirates (for high damage output)

## Special Features

* High damage output with decent attack speed
* Some axes provide bonus critical rate
* Certain axes have bleed effects
* Often used for mobbing due to wide attack range
* Can be used with power strike skills

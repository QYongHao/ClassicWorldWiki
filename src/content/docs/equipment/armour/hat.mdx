Hats are head armor pieces that provide defense and often include special visual effects. They come in various styles and tiers.

## Equipment Stats

* **Defense:** 10~50
* **Req. Level:** 10~200 (varies by tier)
* **Slots:** 1
* **Set Bonuses:** Often part of armor sets

## Class Usage

Hats are used by all classes:
* Warriors (for high defense)
* Magicians (for INT boosts)
* Archers (for DEX boosts)
* Thieves (for LUK boosts)
* Pirates (for balanced stats)

## Special Features

* Some hats provide bonus stats when equipped with other set items
* Certain hats have unique visual effects
* High-end hats may provide skill bonuses
* Some hats provide resistance to status effects

Capes are back armor pieces that provide defense and often include special visual effects. They are highly customizable and sought after.

## Equipment Stats

* **Defense:** 5~40
* **Req. Level:** 10~200 (varies by tier)
* **Slots:** 1
* **Set Bonuses:** Often part of armor sets

## Class Usage

Capes are used by all classes:
* Warriors (for additional defense)
* Magicians (for INT boosts)
* Archers (for DEX boosts)
* Thieves (for LUK boosts)
* Pirates (for balanced stats)

## Special Features

* Many capes have unique visual effects
* Some capes provide bonus stats when equipped with other set items
* High-end capes may provide skill bonuses
* Certain capes provide resistance to status effects
* Can be transparent to show character outfits

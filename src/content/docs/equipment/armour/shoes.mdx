Shoes provide foot protection and often include movement speed bonuses. They are essential for all classes to enhance mobility.

## Equipment Stats

* **Defense:** 10~45
* **Req. Level:** 10~200 (varies by tier)
* **Slots:** 1
* **Movement Speed:** +5~+20
* **Jump:** +5~+15

## Class Usage

Shoes benefit all classes:
* Warriors (for defense and mobility)
* Magicians (for INT and movement)
* Archers (for DEX and mobility)
* Thieves (for LUK and speed)
* Pirates (for balanced stats)

## Special Features

* Most shoes provide movement speed bonuses
* Some shoes increase jump height
* High-end shoes may provide skill bonuses
* Often part of complete armor sets
* Certain shoes provide additional evasion

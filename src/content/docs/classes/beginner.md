# Beginner

## Starting Stats
- Level 1
- 4 STR, 4 DEX, 4 INT, 4 LUK
- 12 HP, 12 MP

## Skills
- Three Snails
- Recovery
- Nimble Feet

## First Job Advancement
- Available at Level 10
- Choose from Warrior, Magician, Bowman, Thief, or Pirate
